About

I run consulting at SMP Solutions in Budapest. I work mostly with banks.

Sixteen years in the consulting end of financial services, the last six of them spent largely on how AI gets — and does not get — into regulated institutions.

I lead the consulting practice at SMP Solutions, a Hungarian advisory firm working primarily with banks in the CEE region. Most of my time goes to a small number of long engagements where the questions are not which model to buy but how the institution should change in order to use it well.

I came into financial services by way of regulatory work — first inside a Big Four firm in Budapest and Vienna, then, briefly, on the supervisor side — before moving fully to the bank-facing consulting side in the late 2010s. The work I have enjoyed most, consistently, has been at the intersection of new technology and old institutions: the moment when a senior team sees the technical possibility, has to decide whether to commit, and has to design the governance scaffolding to make the commitment survivable.

What I focus on

AI governance frameworks that survive contact with an actual supervisor. Generative AI deployments in mid-office banking functions where the ROI math is real. Model-risk management as it applies — and does not apply — to foundation models. Vendor and concentration risk for the AI stack. The specific texture of CEE banking, which is different from Western European banking in ways that matter for technology adoption.

What I do not write about

Anything that requires a confidential anecdote from a current client. Hot takes on the latest model release. Crypto. Generic AI-and-society commentary. I try to write only about things I have, in some form, watched up close. The price of that constraint is that I write rarely. The benefit is that what is here is, I hope, worth your time.

Életrajz

Budapesti Corvinus Egyetem, MSc, pénzügy, 2010. CFA charterholder 2014 óta. ICA-certifikáció MI-kormányzásból és MI-kockázatkezelésből. Magyarul és angolul anyanyelvi szinten beszélek, németül és lengyelül használhatóan, és olvasom a francia szabályozói sajtót, mert oldalanként máig a legjobb Európában.

This site is the public face of work that is otherwise mostly private. If you would like to discuss something specific, write to me.
